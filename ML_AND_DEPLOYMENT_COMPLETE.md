## 🤖 **6 ML FEATURES ADDED**

### Feature 1: Personalized Recommendations ⭐
- AI analyzes budget and preferences
- Scores items on 120-point system
- Shows top 10 recommendations
- Explains why each is recommended

### Feature 2: Smart Price Prediction 💰
- Predicts optimal order times
- Shows potential savings
- Trends analysis
- Confidence levels

### Feature 3: Smart Sorting 📊
- 5 intelligent sort methods:
  - Best (overall quality + price + speed)
  - Cheapest (lowest price)
  - Fastest (quickest delivery)
  - Best Quality (highest rated)
  - Trending (popular items)

### Feature 4: Diet Suggestions 🥗
- 5 diet types:
  - Vegan (plant-based only)
  - Keto (low-carb focus)
  - Protein (high protein)
  - Light (low calorie)
  - Comfort (indulgent foods)

### Feature 5: Demand Forecasting 🚀
- Predicts item popularity
- 4 demand levels:
  - Very High
  - High
  - Medium
  - Low

### Feature 6: Bundle Suggestions 🎁
- Recommends complementary items
- Example: Pizza → Fries + Drink + Dessert
- Shows total price
- Increases average order value
