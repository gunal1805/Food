## 📸 SCREENSHOT GUIDE

### PART 1: GitHub Account

**Step 1: Go to GitHub**
```
URL: https://github.com/
```

**Step 2: Click "Sign up"**
- Top right button
- Or click "Sign up" in the middle

**Step 3: Enter Email**
- Type your email address
- Verify email from GitHub

**Step 4: Create Password**
- Strong password recommended
- Remember it!

**Step 5: Choose Username**
- Something like: `ggs-boys-food`
- Or: `yourusername`
- Must be unique

**Step 6: Complete Signup**
- Answer verification questions
- Verify email address
- Done! ✅

---

### PART 2: Create Repository

**Step 1: After Login**
- You'll be on GitHub dashboard

**Step 2: Click "+"** (top right)
```
+ → New repository
```

**Step 3: Fill Repository Info**
```
Repository name: ggs-boys-website
Description: GGS Food Services - Food Price Comparison
Public: YES (must be public)
```

**Step 4: Click "Create repository"**
- Green button at bottom

**Step 5: You'll see new repository page**
- Empty repo with instructions
- Don't worry about commands
- We'll use web upload!

---

### PART 3: Upload Files to GitHub

**Step 1: On Repository Page**
- You'll see buttons at top
- Look for: "Add file" or "Upload files"
- Click "Upload files" or "Add files" dropdown

**Step 2: Choose Upload Method**
- Click "upload files" (drag & drop)
- Or "choose your files"

**Step 3: Select All Your Files**
**Option A - Drag & Drop (EASIEST):**
- Open File Explorer
- Navigate to: `c:\Users\HP\OneDrive\Desktop\ggs-boys-website\`
- Select ALL files and folders
- Drag to GitHub upload box
- Drop!

**Option B - Click to Select:**
- Click "choose your files"
- Navigate to your folder
- Select multiple files (Ctrl+A)
- Click "Open"

**Step 4: Wait for Upload**
- Progress bar shows upload
- Usually takes 30-60 seconds

**Step 5: Commit Changes**
- Scroll down
- Click green "Commit changes" button
- Message auto-filled
- Done! ✅

**Step 6: All Files Uploaded**
- Refresh page (F5)
- You'll see all your files!
- Check: backend/, css/, js/ folders exist

---

### PART 4: Deploy on Railway

**Step 1: Go to Railway**
```
URL: https://railway.app/
```

**Step 2: Click "Create Project"**
- Large button on homepage
- Or "New Project"

**Step 3: Select GitHub**
- Click "Deploy from GitHub"
- Or "GitHub"

**Step 4: Connect GitHub**
- Click "Connect GitHub"
- You'll login to GitHub again
- Click "Authorize Railway"
- Allow Railway to access your repos

**Step 5: Select Repository**
- After authorization
- Choose: `ggs-boys-website`
- Click it!

**Step 6: Click Deploy/Create**
- Green button at bottom
- Railway will start deployment
- Status shows: "Deploying..."

**Step 7: WAIT 2-3 MINUTES** ⏳
- Don't close page
- Don't refresh
- Just wait...
- Status changes to "Success" or "Running"

---

### PART 5: Get Your Permanent URL

**Step 1: Deployment Completes**
- Status shows "Running" ✅
- Green checkmark appears

**Step 2: Find Your URL**
- Click on the deployment/service
- Look for "Domains" section
- Or scroll for "URL"
- Click "View deployment"
- You'll see your URL

**Step 3: Your URL Looks Like:**
```
https://ggs-food-services-production.railway.app
```

**Step 4: Copy the URL**
- Click to copy
- Save it somewhere
